Visiting San Diego
By Amber

San Diego is a beautiful and diverse city in California . It is known for its pristine beaches, warm climate, and incredible attractions. The city is also close to Los Angeles as they are about a 2 and a half hour drive apart. If you would like to visit Hollywood, the Walk of Fame, and the raving nightlife in Los Angeles, why not stop by San Diego after for a more slow-paced, relaxed vacation? These are the top reasons why you should visit San Diego:

  • The beaches there have breathtaking cliffs, secluded coves, stunning shores, and vibrant restaurants/nightlife. The clear water, sandy shores, and sunny climate make the beaches there the ideal way to spend an afternoon.
  • The San Diego region is known for its mild year-round Mediterranean climate, perfect for those who prefer a warmer temperature.
  • There are attractions in the city that are perfect for families, solo travellers, and any visitors! The world famous San Diego Zoo, for example, home to over 12 000 animals, is renowned for its naturalistic and lush habitats. For history lovers, there is also the San Diego Natural History Museum, which you cannot miss visiting!
